Consumer Risk Disclosure

Borrowing money through short-term and installment loans carries financial risks. High-interest loans may not be suitable for all borrowers, and failure to repay may result in additional fees, collection efforts, or negative impacts on your credit score. Before accepting any loan offer, carefully review the lender’s terms, repayment schedule, and total loan cost to ensure it aligns with your financial situation. If you are facing financial hardship, consider exploring alternative lending options or consulting a financial advisor.

APR Variation Notice

The Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for loans varies depending on lender policies, state regulations, creditworthiness, and loan terms. APRs typically range from 5.99% to 35.99%, but actual APRs may be higher or lower depending on lender policies, loan duration, borrower creditworthiness, state regulations, and additional factors. Lenders are required by law to disclose the exact APR, all fees, repayment schedules, and total loan cost before you agree to the loan.
