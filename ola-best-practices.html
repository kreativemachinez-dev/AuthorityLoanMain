Digital Consulting Group LLC is committed to upholding the highest standards of transparency, ethical lending, and regulatory compliance as set forth by the Online Lenders Alliance (OLA). As a marketing and lead generation platform, we connect consumers with a network of reputable lenders while ensuring adherence to industry best practices.

Find Loan Options

The Online Lenders Alliance (OLA) was established to create a code of conduct for the online lending industry, allowing consumers to identify trustworthy companies. To be an OLA member, companies must comply with its Best Practices, which include:

  • Providing full disclosure of loan terms, including annual percentage rates (APRs), fees, repayment terms, and applicable penalties, in a clear and accessible format.
  • Offering a reasonable loan cancellation policy that allows borrowers to withdraw from a loan within a defined period, subject to lender terms.
  • Prohibiting misleading or deceptive marketing practices, including false claims such as “Guaranteed Approval” or “Instant Funding.”
  • Ensuring compliance with federal and state lending laws, including the Truth in Lending Act (TILA), Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA), Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA), and other consumer protection regulations.
  • Protecting consumer privacy and data security, following regulations such as the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable data protection laws.
  • Processing loan payments only with the borrower’s authorization, ensuring compliance with federal laws governing electronic transactions.
  • Treating consumers fairly and with respect, enforcing ethical lending and debt collection practices within our network.

A full list of OLA Best Practices can be reviewed on the OLA’s official website: https://www.onlinelendersalliance.org/.

Lender Monitoring and Consumer Complaint Process

Digital Consulting Group LLC actively monitors lenders within its network to ensure compliance with OLA standards. Our monitoring process includes:

  • Periodic compliance audits of lenders to verify adherence to ethical lending practices.
  • Customer feedback mechanisms that allow consumers to report lender behavior.
  • Immediate review of complaints related to unfair lending practices, with corrective actions taken when necessary.

Consumers who wish to file a complaint about a lender can do so by contacting us directly. Each complaint is investigated, and if a lender is found to be non-compliant, they may face penalties, removal from our network, or other corrective actions.

Our Role in Compliance and Consumer Protection

Although Digital Consulting Group LLC is not a lender and does not make credit decisions, we take proactive steps to ensure that all lenders within our network meet OLA’s best practices. Our compliance measures include:

  • Regular monitoring of participating lenders to ensure their adherence to ethical lending standards and consumer protection laws.
  • Requiring lenders to disclose full loan terms before a consumer agrees to a loan.
  • Providing consumers with the ability to opt out of marketing communications and data sharing, in compliance with privacy regulations.
  • Ensuring consumers have access to loan details before finalizing any agreement, promoting transparency in lending.

If you believe that a lender in our network has violated fair lending practices or engaged in deceptive conduct, we encourage you to report the issue promptly.

Consumer Risk Disclosure

Borrowing money through short-term and installment loans carries financial risks. High-interest loans may not be suitable for all borrowers, and failure to repay may result in additional fees, collection efforts, or negative impacts on your credit score. Before accepting any loan offer, carefully review the lender’s terms, repayment schedule, and total loan cost to ensure it aligns with your financial situation. If you are facing financial hardship, consider exploring alternative lending options or consulting a financial advisor.

APR Variation Notice

The Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for loans varies depending on lender policies, state regulations, creditworthiness, and loan terms. APRs typically range from 5.99% to 35.99%, but actual APRs may be higher or lower depending on lender policies, loan duration, borrower creditworthiness, state regulations, and additional factors. Lenders are required by law to disclose the exact APR, all fees, repayment schedules, and total loan cost before you agree to the loan.
